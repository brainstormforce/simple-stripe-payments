BSF Form To Google Sheet is a simple form send its form data to google shpreed sheet and send email to admin and recepient.

== Description ==

BSF Form To Google Sheet has two form fields, 1.Name 2.Email. Using this you can easily get backup of your data to your google spreadsheet. Also get email for every form submission.
